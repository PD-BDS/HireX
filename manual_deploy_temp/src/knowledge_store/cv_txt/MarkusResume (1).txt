MARKUS
Chief Mechanical Engineer


Summary
======

Highly experienced Chief Mechanical / Field Engineer with over 25 years of global expertise in mechanical installation, commissioning, maintenance, and plant upgrades for the cement industry and heavy industrial sectors. Proven track record of leading complex projects across Europe, Asia, Africa, and Latin America, with extensive work on kilns, clinker coolers, vertical roller mills (Atox, OK), ball mills, preheaters, CF silos, material handling systems, and associated mechanical equipment.
Brings a strong background in supervising complete EPC cement plant installations, retrofits, and preventive maintenance, with hands-on experience in managing large-scale mechanical assemblies, troubleshooting, and performance optimization. Skilled in welding technology (European Welding Specialist), metallbau (engineering design metal work), and multidisciplinary coordination to ensure safe, timely, and high-quality project delivery.
Internationally exposed, Markus has worked with leading OEMs including FLSmidth, Aumund Fördertechnik GmbH, Gebr. Pfeiffer AG, and MAN GHH, executing assignments ranging from new plant builds to complex retrofits in diverse operating environments. Fluent in German and conversant in English and Spanish, he is adept at working in multicultural teams and challenging site conditions.
