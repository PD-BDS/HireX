Summary
======
Site Manager & Electrical Engineer with 27 years industry experience including back-office project management, site management and lead engineer responsibility. With an MSc in Construction Project Management and a BEng (Hons) in Electronic and Electrical Engineering, I am a versatile and resourceful project team leader and team member. Experienced in all aspects of EPC construction management, electrical engineering and project management of power plants as Owner's Engineer, from feasibility study creation and review; bid document preparation; bidder evaluation and selection; contract negotiation; basic and detailed design review; and site supervision through all aspects of construction and commissioning. I am a skilled writer able to produce a variety of high-quality technical and commercial documents, reports, and specifications. My work on Build, Operate, Transfer (BOT) EPC projects has involved negotiations with various government departments and ministries on contracts such as Power Purchase Agreements (PPA), Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), BOT contracts, Metering Agreements and Grid Connection Agreements.
