GBENGA


Summary
======
Gbenga is a seasoned Electrical and Automation Engineer with extensive experience in the cement industry across Africa and the Middle East. He has played key roles in commissioning, troubleshooting, and automation support for major cement plants such as Dangote, BUA, and Prime Cement. His expertise includes PLC programming, SCADA development, and control systems, with a strong focus on vertical roller mills, as well as hydraulic, lubrication, and pneumatic systems. Gbenga brings critical thinking and hands-on problem-solving to complex industrial environments, consistently ensuring smooth operations and optimized plant performance.
