Summary

Dimitry Levin is a highly skilled Civil Engineer with over 15 years of experience in managing civil infrastructure and renewable energy projects across various sectors, including roads, wind farms, earthworks, and drainage systems. Known for his expertise in bulk earthworks, concrete foundations, procurement, and contract management, Dimitry has successfully led complex projects from inception to completion, ensuring compliance with regulations and high-quality standards. He is proficient in AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Primavera, and MS-Project, with strong capabilities in planning, scheduling, and cost control. Dimitry is a results-driven leader, recognized for his ability to manage multidisciplinary teams, collaborate with stakeholders, and deliver projects on time and within budget.
