Summary
======

A Marine Engineer and certified PRINCE2 Project Manager with over 15 years of diverse experience across sales, service, consulting, and technical project management. He has a proven track record of leading complex national and international projects, particularly in the wind energy sector at Siemens Gamesa, where he served as Product Owner and Technical Project Manager for advanced offshore lifting and engineering equipment.
His career spans roles as project manager, consultant, and service technician, combining strong mechanical and electrical expertise with hands-on industry knowledge. Danni excels in stakeholder management, technical product ownership, process optimization, and implementing innovative solutions that enhance equipment performance and operational efficiency.
With a unique blend of technical insight, commercial acumen, and leadership skills, he is passionate about driving collaboration, solving challenges, and delivering projects that create measurable value and long-term impact.
