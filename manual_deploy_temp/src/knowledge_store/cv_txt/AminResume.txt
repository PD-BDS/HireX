Summary
======

Seasoned Automation and Artificial Intelligence specialist with over 35 years of international experience in cement and process industry automation, expert systems, and advanced quality control solutions. Holder of a PhD in Artificial Intelligence from Université Pierre & Marie Curie, Paris, with a strong background in expert systems development and implementation.
Since 1994, has built a distinguished career at FLSmidth Automation, progressing from system engineer to product manager and global service engineer. Extensive expertise in FLSmidth products including QCX Robolab, QCX BlendExpert, ECS, and PlantGuide, covering full lifecycle delivery—from product development, system integration, commissioning, and upgrades to client support, training, and preventive maintenance.
Proven track record in leading projects across more than 30 countries and 65+ cement and industrial sites, providing hands-on commissioning, remote troubleshooting, and customer support. Skilled in problem diagnosis, system optimization, and developing robust automation solutions for global clients.
