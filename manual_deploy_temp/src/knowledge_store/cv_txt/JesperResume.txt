JESPER
Marine Engineer


Summary
======

An experienced Marine Engineer and Maintenance Manager with over 25 years of expertise spanning mechanical engineering, process plant assembly, maintenance management, and welding. His career reflects a strong technical foundation combined with proven leadership in optimizing operations, enhancing plant uptime, and implementing structured maintenance systems.
Through roles ranging from hands-on fitter and machinist to Maintenance Manager and Senior Service Engineer, Jesper has developed deep capabilities in troubleshooting, repair, commissioning, and customer training across diverse industries including manufacturing, process plants, and offshore environments.
He holds advanced welding certifications, occupational health and safety training, and extensive knowledge of electrical, automation, and process systems. Jesper is also proficient in AutoCAD, Inventor, and the Microsoft Office suite, and communicates effectively in Danish (native), English, and with working understanding of German, Norwegian, and Swedish.
Recognized for his adaptability, problem-solving skills, and ability to deliver reliable technical solutions, Jesper continues to support organizations through service, supervision, and consultancy assignments worldwide.
