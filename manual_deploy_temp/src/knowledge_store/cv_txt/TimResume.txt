TIM
Erection Engineer


Summary
======
A highly experienced Mechanical Supervisor and Senior Site Manager with over two decades of international expertise in supervising mechanical erection, commissioning, maintenance, and modernization of complex industrial plants — primarily in the cement, mining, steel, and power sectors. With a career spanning multiple global assignments, Tim has demonstrated exceptional leadership in managing large-scale construction and mechanical projects across Europe, North and South America, Africa, and Asia.Throughout the career, he has worked with industry leaders such as Plant Supervision, KHD Humboldt, and Loesche GmbH, contributing to the successful execution of a wide range of projects involving kilns, coolers, furnaces, preheaters, grinding systems, vertical roller mills, and high-pressure grinding rolls (HPGR). He has deep technical knowledge in both cold and hot commissioning, refurbishment, and process optimization of plant equipment.
