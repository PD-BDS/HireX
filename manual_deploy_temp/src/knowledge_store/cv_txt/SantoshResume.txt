SANTOSH


Summary
======
Santosh Kumar is a seasoned Electrical, Instrumentation & Control professional with 16 years of extensive experience specializing in electrical installations, commissioning, and maintenance across diverse industrial sectors, particularly cement manufacturing. He has demonstrated expertise in managing projects involving control panels, wiring diagrams, PLC systems, SCADA, and various industrial instruments. Santosh has considerable hands-on experience in electrical installations and pre-commissioning activities for heavy industrial equipment including limestone stackers, reclaimers, coal mills, additive reclaimers, VRMs, pyro-process equipment, ball mills, and packing plants. His specific skills include detailed knowledge of PLC programming (SIMATIC S7, RS Logix), HMI configurations, variable frequency drives (Siemens and ABB), and proficiency in various communication protocols (Profibus, Modbus, Fieldbus). Santosh is highly skilled in conducting Factory Acceptance Tests (FAT), ensuring strict adherence to quality and safety standards. Throughout his career, he has successfully managed numerous electrical and instrumentation commissioning projects internationally, demonstrating leadership and excellence in troubleshooting complex control system issues, optimizing operational efficiency, and ensuring compliance with technical and safety specifications.
