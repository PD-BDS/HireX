JOHN


Summary
======

John is a highly experienced Electrical Supervision Engineer with over four decades of expertise in electrical installations, commissioning, and supervision across diverse industries, including cement, pulp & paper, offshore energy, and industrial manufacturing.
With a strong foundation in electrical engineering from Aalborg University, John has led and contributed to complex projects worldwide, working with major industry leaders such as Valmet, GEA, FLSmidth, and KHD. His extensive experience spans PLC programming (Siemens, Allen Bradley), frequency drives, UPS systems, generators, MV/LV motors, and advanced troubleshooting
