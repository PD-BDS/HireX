PER
Technical Project Manager


Summary
======

A seasoned Technical Project Manager and Industrial Project Management Consultant with over 25 years of international experience leading large-scale Capex projects across the energy, cement, pulp & paper, offshore wind, and food & pharma industries. He has successfully managed global projects with budgets exceeding 600 MEUR, overseeing the full project lifecycle including contract management, scope, cost, risk, QHSE, stakeholder engagement, and team leadership.
Per has held senior roles at Liquid Wind, Valmet AB, Chr. Hansen, Ørsted (Dong Energy), B&W Vølund, and FLSmidth, and is the founder of Alfa-Projektledelse, a consultancy specializing in industrial Capex projects, operational excellence, and PMO setup. His expertise spans portfolio management, cost optimization, governance frameworks, and implementation of PMO tools and project models to drive efficiency and compliance.
With a background in Chemical Engineering (MSc, DTU) and certifications in Project Management (PMP), FIDIC contracts, and cross-cultural leadership, Per combines strong technical knowledge with strategic project leadership. He is fluent in Danish and English, with working proficiency in German and Swedish, and has collaborated extensively with multinational teams across Europe, Asia, and the Americas.
Recognized for his ability to transform organizations from reactive to proactive project execution, Per brings a track record of delivering complex projects on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of safety and quality, while building sustainable stakeholder relationships.
