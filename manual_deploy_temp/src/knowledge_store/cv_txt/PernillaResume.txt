PERNILLA


Summary
======
Pernilla Ebberyd has over ten years of experience in the construction and real estate industry and has been working as a construction and project manager at WSP in Uppsala since 2023. Over the years, she has gained experience in many project types and phases, different forms of contracting and compensation, and from various roles in project organizations for both private and public clients. Pernilla primarily distinguishes herself through her great commitment and drive in everything she does. This includes managing both individual large projects and running several parallel projects, from initiation to implementation and handover. She has led projects in new construction of hospitals, industries, preschools and housing as well as renovations of municipal buildings and commercial kitchens. Her ability to plan and deliver accurate financial and situational reconciliations ensures that the projects follow budget and schedule. Pernilla's drive has also taken her to high levels in sports, where she has played football at an elite level for many years. Through her holistic view and a strategic, structured and results-oriented approach, Pernilla handles complex issues through very good collaboration skills with requirement setters, designers, contractors, users/tenants and management. Pernilla is very communicative in both speech and writing and works both independently and in close collaboration with others in the client organization. All to ensure that projects are implemented according to established technical, financial, time and sustainability frameworks
