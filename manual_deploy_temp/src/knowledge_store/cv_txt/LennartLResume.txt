LENNART L


Summary
======
A globally experienced Project and Site Manager with over 25 years in food and chemical processing installations, including GEA Falling Film evaporators. Skilled in mechanical installation, supervision, and commissioning, ensuring smooth project execution. Lennart has worked in Asia, North and South America, and Europe, bringing strong cultural adaptability and leadership. He has overseen installations in Thailand, Sweden, Ireland, and Singapore, managing equipment reception, component hoisting, crew leadership, IO testing, and cold commissioning. Proficient in P&ID diagrams, 2D/3D layouts, AutoCAD, and Navisworks, he ensures efficient project workflows and seamless handovers. His expertise in evaporators and spray dryers contributes to shorter commissioning periods and optimized installation efficiency. Founder of LCS Consultancy Services ApS, specializing in turnkey process engineering installations, project planning, budget control, and stakeholder management.
