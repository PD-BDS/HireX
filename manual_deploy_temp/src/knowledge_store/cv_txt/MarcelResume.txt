MARCEL
Electrical Engineering Technician


Summary
======

A highly accomplished Electrical Engineering Technician with over four decades of international experience in electrical and instrumentation (E&I) systems, assembly supervision, and site management across power generation, industrial, offshore, and energy sectors. Marcel has an extensive background leading and executing electrical installations, commissioning, loop checks, instrumentation hook-ups, and maintenance of MV/LV switchgears, drives, control systems, and complex automation technologies.
Recognized for his technical expertise and leadership in overseeing large-scale projects across Europe, the Middle East, Latin America, and Asia, he has worked with renowned companies including Siemens, GE, Tenova, ABB, Rolls-Royce, and EPC Ferrostaal. Marcel is particularly skilled in managing electrical erection and instrumentation works on gas and steam turbines, solar power stations, FPSOs, and offshore platforms, as well as implementing quality assurance and compliance processes (ECCs, FQPs).
