DARWIN


Summary
======
Darwin is a seasoned Mechanical Engineer with over 30 years of international experience in supervising the erection, installation, and commissioning of equipment for cement, power, and pulp plant projects. He has worked across more than 15 countries, leading multidisciplinary teams on large-scale industrial installations for clients such as FLSmidth, Valmet, and Lafarge. Darwin is highly skilled in rotary kiln assembly, ESP systems, mechanical commissioning, and managing complex project logistics. He is known for his hands-on leadership, meticulous attention to detail, and ability to ensure quality execution under tight timelines. His extensive field experience and technical knowledge make him a reliable asset for any industrial construction project worldwide.
