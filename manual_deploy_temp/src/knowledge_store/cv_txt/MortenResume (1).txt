MORTEN
Commissioning Engineer


Summary
======
A highly experienced and versatile electrical commissioning engineer with over two decades of hands-on experience across a wide range of electrical installation, automation, and quality assurance projects. His expertise spans industrial, commercial, and residential sectors, with a proven track record in project coordination, PLC troubleshooting, BMS/CTS systems, and electrical supervision in both new constructions and renovations. Has held leadership roles as a foreman on numerous high-profile projects, including work with Siemens Wind Power, Aalborg University, and major infrastructure sites such as Super sygehuset Aalborg and district heating facilities. Morten’s background includes specialized training in building automation systems, digital project coordination, and construction management, complemented by strong skills in quality control, fault diagnosis, and system integration.
