Summary
======
An experienced Field Service Technician and Site Manager with over 35 years of international expertise in the installation, commissioning, maintenance, troubleshooting, and revamping of complex industrial machinery and production systems. His career spans diverse industries, including mechanical engineering, packaging machinery, power transmission, semiconductor technology, refineries, automotive, wire and tube mills, and defense equipment. As a Site Manager, I have successfully directed large-scale international projects, managing multicultural teams, overseeing installation and start-up operations, and ensuring adherence to strict cost and time controls using project management tools such as Microsoft Project. I am skilled in operator training, technical testing, client liaison, and providing solution feedback to support continuous improvement.
