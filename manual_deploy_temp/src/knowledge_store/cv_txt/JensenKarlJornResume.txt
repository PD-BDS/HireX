JENSEN, KARL JØRN
Erection Engineer


Summary
======
A highly experienced Mechanical Erection Engineer with over six decades of hands-on expertise in cement plant construction, mechanical installation, equipment upgrades, and maintenance supervision. Beginning his career in 1963, Mr. Jensen has contributed to major cement and industrial projects across more than 40 countries, including Denmark, Iran, China, South Africa, Algeria, Brazil, Egypt, and Vietnam.I have been recognized for leadership in kiln, cooler, raw mill, and separator installations and overhauls—often serving as Chief Mechanical Engineer or Erection Manager on high-capacity lines of up to 12,000 TPD. His technical proficiency spans rotary kiln systems, Atox mills, preheater towers, stacker/reclaimers, clinker coolers, and cement grinding systems.
