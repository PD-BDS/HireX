SOEREN
Offshore Installation Manager


Summary
======
Seasoned wind energy professional with over 27 years of global experience in onshore and offshore wind turbine installation, construction management, and technical supervision. A certified machinist and engineer, Soeren Frost has successfully led more than 1,500 wind turbine installations worldwide, collaborating with major industry players such as Vestas, Siemens, GE, Alstom, and Fred. Olsen Windcarrier.Renowned for a solution-driven and safety-first approach, Soeren has held key leadership roles—including Offshore Construction Manager, Client Representative, and CEO—managing projects from mobilization to commissioning. His extensive portfolio includes complex offshore assignments in Europe, Asia, and North America, where he has overseen installation teams of up to 80 personnel and delivered critical infrastructure for large-scale wind farms.With strong competencies in mechanical and electrical systems, high-voltage installations, crane operations, site supervision, and client relationship management, Soeren consistently delivers high-quality outcomes while maintaining strict compliance with safety and operational standards. Fluent in Danish and English, and equipped with broad training certifications including GWO, OPITO, and Siemens-specific programs, he combines hands-on expertise with strategic oversight to drive successful project execution.
