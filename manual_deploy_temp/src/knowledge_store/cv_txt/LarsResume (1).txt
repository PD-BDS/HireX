Summary
======
Experienced and highly skilled Electro Engineer and PLC Specialist with over 35 years of international experience in industrial automation, electrical supervision, commissioning, and maintenance across various sectors, including cement, sawmill, energy, and humanitarian aid. Fluent in Swedish and English, with working proficiency in Norwegian and Danish, Lars has built a distinguished career through key roles with global leaders such as Siemens, ABB, Ericsson, FLSmidth, and UN-affiliated agencies. Specialized in PLC systems (Siemens S5/S7, TIA Portal, PCS7, Safety, WinCC), frequency converters (ABB, SEW Eurodrive), and electrical system design using AutoCAD and other CAD tools. Strong hands-on expertise in commissioning, troubleshooting, and design of industrial systems, combined with solid knowledge of UPS systems, emergency power, instrumentation, and generator maintenance.
