JAIRO
Commissioning Supervisor


Summary
======

A seasoned Consultant and Refractories Specialist with over 45 years of international experience in cement plants, pulp and paper mills, and nickel iron mining operations. He has a strong background in production management, process optimization, quality assurance, commissioning (cold and hot), refractory lining, and technical training.
Throughout his career, Jairo has worked with leading multinationals such as Intercement, Supremo Secil, FLSmidth, and Valmet, where he has successfully supervised operations, commissioning, and plant start-ups across Brazil, Indonesia, and Spain. His expertise spans the management and operation of clinker kilns, lime kilns, calciners, and ore dryers, as well as extensive experience in assembling, commissioning, and operating burners with multiple fuel sources including diesel oil, natural gas, CNG, coal, and heavy oil.
Known for his hands-on technical expertise and leadership, Jairo has played a key role in numerous greenfield projects worldwide, delivering reliable plant start-ups and operational excellence. His career reflects a proven ability to lead multidisciplinary teams, implement new technologies, and ensure efficient, safe, and sustainable industrial operations.
