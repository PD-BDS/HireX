KLAUS SUDAN


Summary
======
Klaus Sudan is a seasoned Mechanical Engineer and Project Manager with over four decades of experience in the installation, commissioning, and management of large-scale industrial projects, primarily in the cement, mining, and material handling industries. He has extensive expertise in managing turnkey projects, vertical roller mills, tube mills, rotary kilns, HPGRs, and conveyor systems, delivering solutions in both greenfield and brownfield projects across Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Americas. Klaus has held key roles as Site Manager, Coordinator, and Supervisor, ensuring successful project execution, from planning and estimations to commissioning and operational handover. His global experience includes collaborations with industry leaders such as KHD, Polysius, Loesche, and FLS.
