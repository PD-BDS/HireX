Summary
======

Andrew Coates is a highly experienced Senior Software Engineer with over 25 years in software development specializing in C++, C#, and CUDA programming. He brings a decade of expertise in GPU computing and parallel programming, with a proven track record of designing and optimizing high-performance systems. Andrew has worked across diverse industries, contributing to advanced solutions in data processing, risk modeling, video/image rendering, and real-time computing.
His career includes roles at Innovative Physics, Digital Vision, Trinity House, PensionsFirst, and TCS Computer Services, as well as extensive freelance consultancy. He has successfully delivered projects involving GPU-accelerated algorithms, video grading and restoration, resilient position tracking systems, and Linux-based high-frequency data processing.
With strong knowledge of low-level CPU/GPU operations, compiler design, and multiple programming languages, Andrew is adept at bridging research concepts into practical, robust applications. He combines technical depth with adaptability, demonstrated through his work on greenfield projects, freelance development, and innovative personal projects in AI simulations and video processing.
