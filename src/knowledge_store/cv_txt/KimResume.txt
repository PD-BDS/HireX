Summary

Experienced Software Engineer with a strong background in the design, simulation, and implementation of communication protocols and embedded systems across the telecommunications and renewable energy sectors. Proven expertise in developing ETSI/CCITT/ITU-specified protocols for multiplexers (RSM, ESM, V5) and switches (ISUP - ISDN User Part), with hands-on experience in protocol simulation using SDL/SDT and the Telelogic TAU toolchain. Strong focus on system documentation, testing, and ensuring compliance with industry standards.
