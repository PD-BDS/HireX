NARAYANA MOORTHY
Production Manager


Summary
======

Highly experienced and technically proficient Senior Process Engineer with over 16 years of global experience in cement plant operations, commissioning, and process optimization. He has consistently demonstrated his expertise across greenfield and brownfield projects in Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and Latin America.
He possesses deep operational knowledge of VRMs, rotary kilns, coolers, ball mills, and various burner systems. His core strengths include production optimization, use of alternative fuels and raw materials (AFR), cost reduction strategies, safety auditing, and plant debottlenecking. Narayana has been instrumental in cold and hot commissioning, process troubleshooting, shutdown planning, and manpower management, while also contributing significantly to energy efficiency improvements and emission assessments (NOx and SOx).
He has successfully led cross-functional teams, trained local engineers, and coordinated with international suppliers for technical proposals and equipment procurement. His leadership in refractory and grinding media management and his commitment to process safety have enhanced plant reliability and productivity
