Construction Manager/Engineer TS I am a dual Citizen, Swiss/Australian, an energetic construction professional with 40 years of construction experience of which 30+ years were spent on major international CAPEX projects. I have a wealth of experience in active quality supervision and correction. Raising and issuing of NCR's but endeavour to avoid errors from happening first. This comes with the knowledge of the associated works such as piling, concrete structures, prestressing, waterproofing and construction chemicals, roads and pavements, Steel structures, substations, small power plants, some mechanical and electrical erection and the planning, supervision, quality control and handover thereof. Furthermore, I acquired experience in the education and training field.
