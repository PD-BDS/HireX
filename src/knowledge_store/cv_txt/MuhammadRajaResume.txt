Summary
======
I hold a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering and possess 11 years of professional work experience. My expertise lies in the field of construction services, with a qualification and competency as an construction occupational safety and health associate expert. My areas of comprehensive understanding include observe and identify hazards, make a program HSE plan, ensure that all work is according to procedures, carry out programs according to regulations, provide education about hazards in the workplace, prevent accidents and ensure that everything is safe. Moreover, I have gained valuable experience in various projects, including oleo chemical project, dam projects, bridge and road construction, cooling tower project, tunnel works chemical project tank and piperack offshore & onshore, substation building during my career. I also got award when I and Margatiga Dam Project Team received the Zero Accident Award for 2020 & 2021 for their achievements in implementing the HSE Program to achieve a total of 1,995,186 manhours of work without work accidents from February 1st 2018 to December 31th 2020 from Kemnaker RI and Achievement the HIV- AIDS Prevention and Control Program Award in the workplace in the GOLD category from Kemnaker RI in 2021
