MICHAEL


Summary
======
Michael is a highly experienced Electrical and Instrumentation Engineer with over 40 years of expertise in industrial electrical systems, instrumentation, commissioning, and project management. His career spans a diverse range of industries, including cement, oil & gas, power plants, and manufacturing, where he has played a pivotal role in designing, installing, testing, and maintaining complex electrical and control systems. Michael has worked on major international projects across Africa, the Middle East, Asia, and Europe, demonstrating a strong ability to lead multidisciplinary teams, manage large-scale maintenance operations, and ensure seamless technology transfer. His expertise includes plant-wide preventive maintenance, troubleshooting, and system optimization, ensuring efficient and reliable industrial operations.
