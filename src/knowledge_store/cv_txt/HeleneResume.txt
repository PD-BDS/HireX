HELENE


Summary
======
Helene is a dedicated Global Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) professional with over 31 years of comprehensive experience in international corporations and innovative projects. Passionate about creating and maintaining safe and healthy workplaces, Helene excels in designing and implementing effective HSE strategies, policies, and initiatives aligned with international standards and local regulations. Her extensive expertise includes risk assessment, incident monitoring and reporting, promoting proactive HSE cultures, and executing engaging communication strategies tailored to diverse audiences. Helene's leadership has significantly improved HSE performance and compliance in various sectors including energy, manufacturing, and logistics. Her achievements span roles in prominent companies like Nel Hydrogen, Elopak, Fiberline Composites, Maersk Oil & Gas, Siemens Windpower, and BP. Helene is known for her positive, driven personality, and strong organizational skills, both professionally and in volunteer capacities, demonstrating her capability in leading teams and complex projects effectively.
