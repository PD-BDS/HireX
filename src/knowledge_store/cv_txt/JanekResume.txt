Summary

Janek Blankensteiner is a business consultant and attorney with extensive experience in business transformation, commercial negotiations, and organisational leadership. With a background in global consulting and legal advisory, he excels at driving change, executing strategy, and leading high-stakes negotiations across industries such as banking, life sciences, and manufacturing. His expertise in organisational design, cultural transformation, and commercial deal execution enables him to create lasting impact by combining legal and business acumen with a people-centric approach. His ability to simplify complex matters, connect with diverse stakeholders, and drive collaboration has made him a trusted leader in both business and legal environments. With a global perspective and a results-driven mindset, he thrives in high-paced, people-focused environments where strategic ambition meets real-world execution.
