VIVI


Summary
======
Vivi is a highly driven and versatile Quality Control Lead Inspector with extensive experience in welding coordination, inspection, and quality assurance across various industries, including manufacturing, construction, and offshore projects. She has in-depth expertise in ISO standards (ISO 3834, ISO 5817, EN 1090, and ISO 9001), welding certification, NDT coordination, and quality management systems like WeldEye. Known for her strong work ethic and dedication to continuous improvement, Vivi excels at team leadership, process optimization, and customer interaction. With a background in vocational teaching and welding support, she has a proven track record of training and certifying welders while ensuring compliance with technical standards and safety protocols.
