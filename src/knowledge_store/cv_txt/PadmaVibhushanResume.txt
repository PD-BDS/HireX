Summary
======
A seasoned professional with over 34 years of extensive experience in cement plant production, operations, commissioning, process optimization, and troubleshooting across Cement, Ready-Mix, and Batching Plants. Proven track record of successfully executing plant commissioning and operations in India, Oman, the UAE (RAK & Dubai), and Qatar, with strong adaptability to diverse multinational cultures and remote locations. Skilled in commissioning and optimizing a wide range of equipment including Kilns, VRMs, Ball Mills, and multi-fuel burners. Extensive hands-on expertise in alternative fuel systems (petroleum sludge, sheared tires, pet coke, municipal waste, heavy oil, gas, coal) and raw materials, as well as advanced systems such as dust suppression, GGBS & fly ash blending, inert gas operations, Gamma Metrics, alkali bypass systems, and refractory/shutdown work. Specialized in VRM roller & table hard-facing and commissioning of multichannel fuel burners.
